Why Become a Donor?

Every blood donation can save up to three lives. Join our community of lifesavers and make a real difference in your area.

  • Someone needs blood every 2 seconds.
  • 1 in 7 people entering a hospital needs blood.
  • There is no substitute for human blood.
See Real Stories

Who Can Donate?

  • Age between 18 and 65 years
  • Minimum weight 50kg
  • Good general health
  • No recent major illness or surgery
