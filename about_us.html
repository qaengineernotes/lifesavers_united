About Lifesavers United

A comprehensive overview of our mission, services, and commitment to saving lives through blood donation support.

Introduction and Mission

Our Foundation

Lifesavers United, founded in 2023, is a dedicated humanitarian organization based in Ahmedabad that is committed to providing urgent and reliable blood donation support. Our core mission is driven by a simple yet powerful motto:

"No life should be lost due to a lack of blood."

We operate on a 24/7 basis, ensuring that immediate blood donation assistance is available to save lives at any hour of the day or night.

Founded in 2023

A young but impactful organization making a difference in Ahmedabad and surrounding areas.

24/7 Operations

Round-the-clock availability to respond to emergency blood donation needs.

Ahmedabad Based

Serving the community with local expertise and immediate response capabilities.

Key Areas of Service

Our services are focused on supporting some of the most vulnerable and critical cases in the Ahmedabad region. We are fully committed to meeting the urgent blood needs of:

Patients with Chronic Illnesses

We provide ongoing support to patients, particularly children, who are battling chronic diseases like cancer and thalassemia, which require frequent blood transfusions.

Emergency and Surgical Patients

We respond immediately to the blood needs of individuals undergoing heart surgery, bone marrow transplants, and those who have been in accidents.

Government and Public Hospitals

We are dedicated to serving patients receiving treatment in government hospitals and those who have traveled to Ahmedabad from other regions, who often face unique challenges in securing blood donations.

The Founder's Vision and Leadership

Meet the visionary leader whose personal commitment and extensive experience in social work has transformed blood donation services in Gujarat, creating a legacy of hope and healing.

Moxesh Shah - Founder & Visionary Leader

A dedicated humanitarian and social entrepreneur, Moxesh Shah has dedicated his life to serving the community through blood donation and social welfare initiatives. His journey from being a regular blood donor to founding Gujarat's largest blood donation network exemplifies his unwavering commitment to saving lives.

Prolific Blood Donor

Over 60 donations as an SDP (Single Donor Platelet) donor, with a track record of saving countless lives through his personal contributions

Extensive Social Work Experience

Active in social work since 2015, with deep involvement in community development, education support, and humanitarian initiatives

Strategic Leadership

Built and manages Gujarat's largest blood donation network, coordinating with hospitals, blood banks, and volunteers across the state

Trustee & Partner

Serves as trustee of Unique Artist Foundation and partner with One Vision Group, demonstrating his commitment to diverse social causes

24/7 Availability

Personally available round-the-clock for emergency blood requests, often responding to calls at any hour to save lives

His Vision

"Humanity first; provide immediate and reliable blood support to every patient."

This guiding principle ensures that every action taken by the organization is rooted in compassion and a profound commitment to saving lives. Moxesh's vision extends beyond just blood donation - it encompasses creating a society where no one suffers due to lack of access to life-saving resources.

His vision drives the organization to continuously innovate, expand reach, and build stronger community networks that can respond to any emergency, anywhere, at any time.

Personal Motto

"As long as someone in need is waiting, I will continue to take the initiative."

This motto is a testament to the unwavering dedication that defines the organization's approach to serving the community. It reflects Moxesh's personal commitment to never turning away from someone in need, regardless of the time, effort, or resources required.

This philosophy has inspired countless volunteers and donors to join the cause, creating a ripple effect of compassion and service throughout Gujarat's communities.

Leadership Philosophy

Lead by Example: Moxesh personally participates in every aspect of the organization's work

Empower Others: He believes in building capacity and leadership in others

Collaborative Approach: Fosters partnerships and teamwork across all initiatives
