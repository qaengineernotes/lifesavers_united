Important Disclaimer

The results from these assessment tools are based on the information you provide and are for general guidance only. They may not be 100% accurate and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with healthcare professionals or blood donation center staff for definitive eligibility determination. Individual circumstances may vary, and final donation eligibility is determined at the donation center.

Maintain Healthy Iron Levels

  • • Eat iron-rich foods: red meat, spinach, beans
  • • Include vitamin C to enhance iron absorption
  • • Avoid coffee/tea with meals (reduces absorption)
  • • Consider iron supplements if recommended by doctor
  • • Wait 90 days (males) or 120 days (females) between whole blood donations to replenish

What to Expect

  • • Registration and health screening (15-20 min)
  • • Hemoglobin test (finger prick)
  • • Blood pressure and temperature check
  • • Donation process (8-10 minutes)
  • • Rest and refreshments (15-20 min)
  • • Total time: about 1 hour
