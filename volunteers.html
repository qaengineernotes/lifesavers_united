A Story That Inspires Us All

Every volunteer has a reason they stepped forward. Here's one story that captures what Lifesavers United is truly about.

Moxesh Shah
Founder & Volunteer
Featured Story
"No life should be lost due to a lack of blood. That one belief changed everything for me — and for thousands of patients across Gujarat."
— Moxesh Shah, Founder

Moxesh began his journey as a regular blood donor and went on to build Gujarat's largest emergency blood donation network. With over 60 SDP donations personally and a 24/7 commitment to every emergency call, he embodies what it means to be a lifesaver. His leadership has inspired hundreds of community volunteers to follow the same path.
