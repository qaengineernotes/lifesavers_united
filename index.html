LifeSavers United is India's free emergency blood donation platform, actively serving Ahmedabad, Nadiad, Surat, Vadodara, Mumbai, Rajkot, and all major cities across Gujarat and India. We connect verified blood donors with patients urgently needing O−, O+, A+, A−, B+, B−, AB+, and AB− blood groups including rare blood types 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Whether you need 1 unit for surgery or multiple units for thalassemia treatment, our donor network typically responds within hours. Register as a donor today or submit an emergency blood request completely free.
